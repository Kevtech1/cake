Privacy Policy

Welcome to Valentines Cake Kenya. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order or create an account.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, payment details are processed securely through third-party payment gateways.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including cookies, IP addresses, and browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Improve our services and personalize your experience.
  • Communicate with you regarding your orders and promotional offers.
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraudulent transactions.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist in payment processing, delivery services, and website hosting.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no online transaction is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and safeguard your login credentials.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
